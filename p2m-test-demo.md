## Expected Output (Following P2M Skill Instructions)

## Scene 1: Seaside Boardwalk Stroll
**Setting:** Wooden boardwalk along a beach, ocean in soft focus background, late afternoon. Model walking casually with sandals in hand, relaxed summer vacation vibe.
**Lighting:** Golden hour sunlight creating warm, soft illumination with gentle shadows. Natural light enhances the sage green color and shows the fabric's natural texture.
**Mood/Atmosphere:** Carefree summer escape, breezy coastal living, effortless vacation style. Conveys relaxation and natural beauty.
**Product Focus:** Linen-cotton blend's lightweight quality evident in how the dress moves with the ocean breeze. A-line silhouette flatters movement, midi length appropriate for beach-to-town transition. Sage green complements natural coastal tones.

## Scene 2: Outdoor Farmers Market
**Setting:** Sunny farmers market with colorful produce stalls and flower vendors in background. Model selecting fresh flowers, reusable market bag over shoulder.
**Lighting:** Bright natural daylight filtered through market canopies, creating dappled light that's soft and flattering without harsh shadows.
**Mood/Atmosphere:** Sustainable lifestyle, community connection, wholesome living. Fresh and approachable summer aesthetic.
**Product Focus:** Breathable linen-cotton fabric perfect for warm-weather market browsing. Side pockets visible and functional (holding market finds). Relaxed fit shows comfort for active daily activities.

## Scene 3: Garden Brunch
**Setting:** Outdoor garden patio with white wrought-iron furniture, greenery and blooming flowers around. Model at small table with fresh juice and pastries, morning setting.
**Lighting:** Soft morning light filtered through garden foliage, creating natural diffused lighting with botanical shadows.
**Mood/Atmosphere:** Leisurely weekend morning, cultivated but relaxed elegance, wellness-focused lifestyle.
**Product Focus:** Adjustable straps and comfortable fit suitable for leisurely brunch. Sage green harmonizes with garden environment while remaining the focal point. Linen blend's natural wrinkle character adds authenticity and lived-in appeal.

## Model Profile
**Appearance:** Western European or American, 25-32 years old, natural sun-kissed healthy glow, approachable features. Medium build that shows the dress's relaxed fit authentically.
**Styling:** Loose beachy waves or casual updo, minimal natural makeup emphasizing fresh skin, simple gold jewelry (delicate necklace or small hoops). Paired with woven sandals or espadrilles. Straw hat or sunglasses as optional accessories.
**Pose/Attitude:** Natural, candid moments - walking, reaching for flowers, sipping juice. Genuine smiles and relaxed expressions. Body language open and easy, not stiff or overly posed.
**Why This Model:** Represents the conscious summer dresser who values natural fibers and timeless silhouettes. Embodies effortless style and sustainable fashion choices appealing to eco-aware consumers seeking versatile warm-weather pieces.
